About us

We are a large clinic located in Norwood, Ontario, serving the surrounding area

We are dedicated to providing the best care to our customers, large and small

We provide a range of services of all types of creatures, including a overnight stay for your pets if needed

We would love to be able to help you and your critters as best we can!

Continue through the site to see the range of services we provide, and meet our team, and book an appointment if needed

Services

We offer a wide range of services to keep your animals happy and healthy:

Service

Description
Routine Checkup: $25-100 Routine checkup to make sure your animal is heathy as can be, and is up to date on all their medications
Vaccine: $100-200 An appointment to give your animal the recommended vaccines
Microchipping: $50 An appointment to give your animal a microchip, to aid in finding them should they get lost
Neuter/Spay: $95-300 An appointment to neuter or spay your pets. Generally requires an overnight stay as well
Overnight Stay: $150 An additional cost, for when your creature requires frequent or high level care for longer then just one day

Note: price ranges are due to the many different types of animals we service, and may not always be accurate for less common animals
